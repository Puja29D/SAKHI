Right to Free Legal Aid

You are entitled to a lawyer free of cost from the Legal Services Authority. Ask the police or court for 'Legal Aid'.

Protection from Harassment

The police must register a 'Zero FIR' regardless of where the incident happened.

Online Reporting

Use the National Cyber Crime Portal or She-Box for work-related complaints anonymously.

One-Stop Centres

Provide medical, legal, and police help under one roof, specifically for women.

Identity Protection

Under Indian law, your identity is protected. Police and media cannot reveal your name.

Medical Privacy

Doctors and hospital staff must keep your records private and strictly confidential.
